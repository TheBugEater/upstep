# Code of conduct

Upstep contributors and maintainers are expected to make participation a
welcoming, harassment-free experience regardless of age, body size, disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity or expression, experience,
education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, caste, color,
religion, or sexual and romantic identity and orientation.

Be respectful, assume good intent, accept constructive feedback and focus on
what is best for users and the community. Harassment, threats, discriminatory
language, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing and sustained
disruption are not acceptable.

Report conduct concerns privately to `hello@upstep.dev`. Maintainers may edit,
remove or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban people
whose behavior is inappropriate. Reports will be handled with discretion and
conflicts of interest will be avoided where practical.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1.
