# Trademark policy

The open-source licenses cover the code, not the Upstep name or logos.

You may use the name “Upstep” to truthfully describe the upstream project,
compatibility, or the origin of unmodified software. You may also state that a
fork is “based on Upstep.”

Please do not use the Upstep name, logo, domains or confusingly similar marks
in a way that suggests an unofficial deployment, fork, product or service is
operated, sponsored or endorsed by Upstep. Forks should use their own product
name and branding unless written permission has been granted.

This policy is intended to protect users from confusion, not to restrict fair
use, commentary or truthful references to the project.
